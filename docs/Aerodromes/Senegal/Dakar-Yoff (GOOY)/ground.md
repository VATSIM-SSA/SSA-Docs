## Taxi Out Procedures

- All IFR Departures should depart from runways 18/36, with aircraft instructed to taxi to holding point S1 (Runway 36 active) or holding point S2 (Runway 18 active) via S and hold short the runway.

### Phraseology

Standard ICAO phraseology.

## Taxi In Procedures

- In low traffic situations, Tower Controllers are expected to hand over arrival traffic to Ground Controllers once the traffic has vacated the runway.
- In high traffic situations, to maintain high-speed exits clear of traffic, Tower Controllers may issue an initial taxi instruction to arrival traffic before handover to Ground Controllers.
