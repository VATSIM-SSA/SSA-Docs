# HKUK Tower

## Runway Selection

- Runway 19 is the preferred active runway, up to 10 kts of tailwind.

## Runway Change Operations

- Runway changes must be coordinated with existing TWR and APP controllers.
- If possible, hold aircraft on stand before a runway change operation.
- If an aircraft is already taxing on one of the apron or terminal taxiways, offer the pilot the option to use the existing active runway or to enter the runway for taxiway direction change.
- If an aircraft has already passed an approach IAF (final turn on pattern or right wide base) fix, an APP or TWR controller should advise the pilot of the possibility of a runway change prior to the aircraft being on the final approach segment.
