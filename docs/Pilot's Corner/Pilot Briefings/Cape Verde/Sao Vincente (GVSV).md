# Sao Vincente Cesaria Evora Airport

*Pilots taking part in events that depart from or arrive at GVSV should make themselves familiar with this briefing page as well as with all existing charts.*

## Overview

- Sao Vincente Cesaria Evora airport is located on the island of Sao Vincente. The island is located in the northwest of the archipelago.

- The airport has runways 06 / 24.

 ## Charts

- GVSV does have publicly available charts located on chartfox.

## Clearance Delivery

- GVSV_TWR will be responsible for Clearance Delivery unless GVSC_DEL is online.
- Expect departure from runway 06. Please comply with initial climb altitudes and departure procedures.

- Oceanic Clearances are not required at Sal Oceanic.

## Ground operations

GVSV_TWR will be the Position responsible for providing push-back and taxiing instructions. 
Remember to squawk Mode C before taxiing. 
If told to monitor a frequency, you're expected to tune in the frequency but not to contact the controller. He will contact you when able. 
When approaching the runway, you'll be asked to report fully ready for departure. That means that when given line-up or take-off clearance, you're expected to be ready to do so without delay. If you need more time, please advise the tower.
