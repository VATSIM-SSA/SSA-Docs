# Tower

The responsibility of Tower at Maputo International Airport falls to the dedicated Tower ATS unit, "Maputo Tower" (FQMA_TWR) on 118.100. TWR will be responsible for the movements on the runway, as well as the responsibility of ensuring safety amongst VFR aircraft operating in the circuit or within the Maputo CTR from GND - 3000ft MSL.


## Visual Flight Rules (VFR) Aircraft

| Type | Circuit Altitude |
| :---------: | :---------: |
| Jet | 1500ft |
| Turbine / Piston | 1000ft |
| Helicopter | 500ft |

 

!!! info "Circuit Clearance"
    C9ABC, hold position, after departure Runway XX, L / R hand circuits, not above XXXXft, report L / R downwind Runway XX.
